  1. Single or various thematic Sites

    Most pages of single thematic sites are dedicated to a specific issue or topic. On the other hand, various thematic sites cover a wide range of information and may be dedicated to a large number of topics and trends. The demand for such sites is great. They are used by both big companies and single users.

  2. News sites

    News sites perform a very important task - to convey to the user various news that happened, will happen or are happening at the moment. However, news sites can be dedicated to a specific topic or tell the Internet community news of different scopes of human life.

  3. Blogs

    In the nowadays World Wide Web blogs are common phenomenon, while the level of their popularity is growing. Blogs, as well as news sites are designed to convey a particular information to the online community. However, with one significant difference: the author describes his personal opinion on what is happening.

  1. Corporate sites

    Corporate Internet resources are created by organizations or companies to perform one of two specific objectives: strengthening communication between company’s branches or improving reputation in the sight of potential and existing customers.

  2. Business card sites

    Business card site have unusual structure. They are divided into several sections, that contain information about the company that sells the products, services that are provided and contact information. .

  3. Portfolio sites

    Portfolio websites are designed to attract the attention of wide target audience by advertising the work performed by an organization or company. The client makes a conclusion based on the concrete result. They are very popular among photo studios, web studios and developers.

  1. Showcase sites

    The main purpose of showcase site is not the sale the product, but only its advertising. They contain only detailed information about the product and sometimes the place where you can buy it. Thy are the most popular among companies and enterprises that produce goods.

  2. Promo sites

    Promo sites not only promote certain product or service, but pay special attention to their benefits. They are characterized by a fairly simple structure, contain various graphics and contact information.

  3. Online stores

    Online stores are designed to sell products of different categories on network online. By visiting this site, every user of the web can choose product they are looking for, find out the details and, if necessary, make orders from home..

  1. Forums

    Forums are sites specialized for users to freely discuss different news, issues, life scopes of society ect.

  1. Catalogues

    Catalogs are sites that contain structured links to other Web resources and their brief description.
